About Us

Who are we?

At Events by Ray founded by Daniel Onyinye Emmanuella. We’re more than just event planners we’re memory-makers. Founded with a passion for bringing joy to every moment, we’ve helped countless clients host stress-free, stylish, and meaningful events.
What started as a simple love for decorating and organizing small gatherings quickly grew into a full-scale brand driven by creativity, reliability, and excellence. Whether it's an intimate dinner, a grand wedding, or a corporate launch, our goal is always the same: to create an experience that leaves a lasting impression.
We believe that every event tells a story and we’re here to help you tell yours with elegance, precision, and heart. From concept to clean-up, our team brings fresh ideas, trusted vendors, and hands-on coordination to ensure everything goes off without a hitch.
With Events by Ray, you don’t just get an event you get peace of mind, personalized service, and unforgettable moments.
